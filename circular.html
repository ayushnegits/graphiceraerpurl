Project Submission Guidelines – B.Tech Final Year (2025–26)
📅 5 Mar 2026 🏛 Department of CSE 📌 Ref: GEU/CSE/2026/08
Academic
›

All B.Tech 4th Year students (Batch 2022–2026) are required to submit their Major Project reports as per the following guidelines:

  • The project report must be submitted in both hard copy and soft copy (CD) to the respective Department.
  • Report format must strictly follow the GEU dissertation template (available on portal).
  • Hard Copy Submission Deadline: 30 April 2026
  • Project Viva Voce: 5–10 May 2026
  • Anti-plagiarism certificate (Turnitin) is mandatory. Similarity index must be below 20%.

Students failing to submit the project report within the deadline will receive an 'F' grade in the project subject and will not be allowed to appear for viva voce.

Head of Department, Computer Science & Engineering, GEU.

Hostel Room Allotment & Rules – Even Semester 2026
📅 10 Jan 2026 🏛 Hostel Administration 📌 Ref: GEU/HOST/2026/02
Hostel
›

Graphic Era University hostel facilities are available for all enrolled students. Room allotment for the Even Semester 2026 will be done on a first-come-first-served basis.

  • Registration Window: 5–15 January 2026 (Portal: Hostel → Room Registration)
  • Hostel fee must be paid in full before check-in.
  • Students must vacate their rooms within 3 days after the last examination.
  • Guests are not permitted inside hostels after 9:00 PM.
  • Any damage to hostel property will be charged to the concerned student.

Ragging is strictly prohibited. Any incident of ragging should be reported immediately to the anti-ragging committee or the helpline: 1800-180-5522.

Hostel Administration, Graphic Era (Deemed to be) University.
